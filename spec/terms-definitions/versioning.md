[[def: versioning, Versioning, version, versions]]

~ A mechanism broader than just Github.com git versioning. It extends in the KERICONF organization to all documents produced in the process. They should get a clear semantic version number that increments every step.

~ - Versioning guidelines
~ Version 0.x: drafts/concepts, where x is the increment from 1.
~ Version 0.x.y: drafts/concepts, where y is the increment from 1 used to express minor updates like typo corrections
~ Version 1.x: final docs where x is the increment from 1 used to express updates
~ Version 2.: major upgrades of the document.
