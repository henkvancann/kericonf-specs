~ [[ref: entry-level, Entry level, Entry levels]]

~ The level of understanding needed per target group and track to be able to have an optimal learning experience during a conference. 

~ More info: The level should be rudimentary enough to progress a lot during the conference, either through 
~ - completely new knowledge to digest during the conference and in two or three months after the conference, or
~ - repeat and enforce knowledge that's already present in the participants competence to a level of the profiles on the [Careers](https://keri.foundation/careers/) of the KERI Foundation website.
